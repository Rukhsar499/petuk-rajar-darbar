About Us

About Restaurant

We have named our restaurant as Petuk Rajar Darbar because it is ultimately a Bengali cuisine restaurant where you will be served authentic Bengali food prepared in proper Bengali style. We have decked up the ambience of the restaurant so that you can have the vibe of pure Bengali culture.  We are committed to serving you tasty foods with good hospitality where the quality of cooking ingredients is maintained.  We believe in the phrase that good food can make a good mood, so we try to make your weekend memorable by offering you delicious food options.

Cuisine Experience

We offer authentic Bengali preparations where you can have several dishes of fish, chicken, mutton and many more. We deliver the proper Bengali taste in those cuisines.

Our restaurant has an expert chef team who can also provide you with the proper veg meal. We used fresh and seasonal vegetables in those dishes.

In terms of nonveg, we also keep some unique dishes on the weekends so that you can enjoy them with your friends or family.
